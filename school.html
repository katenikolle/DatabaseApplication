Karasuno High School (烏野高校) is a fictional public high school, located in Miyagi Prefecture in Northern Japan. The school is based on the real-life Karumai High School in Iwate Prefecture. It is known for its powerful volleyball team, particularly the boys' team, who were once a national powerhouse but have experienced a period of decline before rebuilding.

Metropolitan Nekoma High School (都立音駒高校) is a fictional high school, located in Nerima Ward of Tokyo. They have a strong, almost familial, bond with Karasuno High School, often referred to as their "destined rival". Nekoma's volleyball team is known for its strategic play and their strong defense, led by their libero, Yaku.

Shiratorizawa Academy (白鳥沢学園) is a fictional private high school, located in Sendai, the capital of Miyagi Prefecture. It is based on real-life Tohoku High School. Shiratorizawa Academy is a prestigious, private high school in Miyagi Prefecture, Japan, known for its strict academic and sports programs, including a strong volleyball team. The school is highly selective, with a challenging entrance exam and a significant focus on both academics and athletics, particularly volleyball.
